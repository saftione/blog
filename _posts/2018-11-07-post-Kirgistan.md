Facts about Kyrgyzstan: 
- 200000 m² ( = <1/2 of Germany )
- 90% above 1500m
- 20% of the area usable for agriculture
- Climate: semiarid

Kyrgyz agriculture
- much old technique 
- Handwork
- improvisation
- cultivation in high altitudes
- mainly irrigated fields
- cultivation of plants for daily needs
